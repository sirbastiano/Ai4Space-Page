Mentorship Program

The workshop will offer a dedicated mentorship program during coffee breaks and lunch periods, creating valuable opportunities for:

  • Research discussions with senior researchers
  • Informal networking with peers and mentors
  • Participating in round table sessions connecting junior and senior researchers

We actively invite senior researchers in the space-AI community to volunteer as mentors.
